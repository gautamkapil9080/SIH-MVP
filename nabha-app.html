🚨 Emergency Warning Signs

Call Emergency Services (108) IMMEDIATELY if you experience:

  • Severe chest pain or pressure
  • Severe difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Loss of consciousness or severe confusion
  • Severe bleeding that won't stop
  • Signs of stroke: Face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty
  • High fever (above 103°F/39.4°C) with severe headache
  • Severe allergic reaction (difficulty breathing, swelling)
  • Severe abdominal pain with vomiting

🌡️ Common Conditions Guide

Fever & Cold

Symptoms: Fever, runny nose, cough, headache

Care: Rest, fluids, paracetamol for fever

See doctor if: Fever above 102°F for more than 3 days

Stomach Issues

Symptoms: Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal pain

Care: Clear fluids, BRAT diet (Banana, Rice, Applesauce, Toast)

See doctor if: Severe dehydration, blood in stool

Respiratory Issues

Symptoms: Cough, congestion, mild breathing difficulty

Care: Steam inhalation, warm fluids, rest

See doctor if: Persistent cough, breathing difficulty

🏥 When to Seek Medical Care

🚨 Emergency (Call 108)

Life-threatening symptoms requiring immediate medical attention

⚡ Urgent (Within 4 hours)

Severe symptoms that need prompt medical care

🔄 Moderate (Within 24 hours)

Concerning symptoms that should be evaluated soon

📅 Low (Within few days)

Mild symptoms that can be monitored at home

🏛️ Government Health Schemes

🎯 Available for Nabha, Punjab Residents

Take advantage of various government health schemes designed to provide affordable healthcare to rural communities.

🏛️ Ayushman Bharat - PM-JAY

Active

Free treatment up to ₹5 lakh per family per year

Eligibility:

Families listed in SECC-2011 database, BPL families

Coverage:

1,393+ medical packages, pre and post hospitalization

Local Hospitals:

Civil Hospital Nabha, Rajindra Hospital Patiala

How to Apply:

Visit nearest CSC or use Ayushman Bharat app

🌾 Punjab Health Insurance Scheme

Active

Free healthcare for all Punjab families below poverty line

Eligibility:

All BPL families in Punjab with valid ration card

Coverage:

Up to ₹2 lakh treatment, medicines, diagnostic tests

Local Centers:

All PHCs, CHCs, District Hospital Patiala

Contact:

District Health Officer, Patiala: 0175-2212345

Eligibility:
